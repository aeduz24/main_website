Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Aeduz ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users ("you" or "your") of our website and services.

1. Collection of Personal Information:

We may collect personal information directly from you when you interact with our website or utilize our services. This information may include but is not limited to:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Location
  • Field of study
  • Age
  • Educational level, etc

2. Purpose of Information Collection:

The personal information we collect is used solely for the purpose of providing personalized mentorship services and facilitating mentor matching based on relevant criteria such as location, academic area, grade level, and age.

3. Information Sharing:

We do not disclose or share personal information with any third parties unless required by law or necessary to comply with legal obligations.

4. Security Measures:

We employ industry-standard security measures to safeguard the confidentiality and integrity of your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Data Retention:

Personal information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected and in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you have any questions or requests regarding your data, please contact us using the information provided below.
